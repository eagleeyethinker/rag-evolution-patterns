WORKSPACE GUIDE
Remote-first teams are expected to be online during core collaboration hours. For most teams, those hours are 10 AM to 3 PM EST.

HOME OFFICE FAQ
The home office stipend is capped at $500 for full-time employees. Typical approved purchases include desks, chairs, monitors, keyboards, and lighting.

PROCUREMENT NOTE
Large furniture purchases should stay within the approved stipend limit unless a manager approves an exception in writing.

TRAVEL POLICY
Employees can expense airport transportation, lodging, and conference meals when they attend approved business travel.

MONITOR GUIDE
External monitors include a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty unless the vendor listing says otherwise.

LAPTOP GUIDE
Company laptops ship with a 3-year AppleCare warranty and should be replaced through IT, not through the home office stipend.
