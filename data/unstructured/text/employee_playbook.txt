REMOTE COLLABORATION
Remote work is available up to 3 days a week for most employees.
Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM EST.
Teams should document decisions in shared channels so remote teammates can catch up asynchronously.

HOME OFFICE PURCHASING
Full-time employees receive a $500 stipend for home office upgrades.
Eligible purchases include standing desks, ergonomic chairs, monitors, webcams, and task lighting.
Employees should submit receipts within 30 days of purchase.

EQUIPMENT LIFECYCLE
Company laptops are covered by a 3-year AppleCare warranty.
External monitors come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty.
IT owns laptop replacements, while employees manage home office accessories purchased with the stipend.
