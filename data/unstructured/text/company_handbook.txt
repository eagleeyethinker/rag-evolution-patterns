COMPANY HANDBOOK 2024

1. Remote Work Policy:
Employees are allowed to work from home up to 3 days a week. Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM EST. 

2. Home Office Budget:
Full-time employees are eligible for a $500 stipend to upgrade their home office setup. This can be used for standing desks, ergonomic chairs, or monitors.

3. Warranty and Equipment:
All company laptops (Product X) come with a 3-year AppleCare warranty. External monitors (Product Y) have a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty.
